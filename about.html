About Mr. Spice Food Truck

Our Story

Mr. Spices began when our founder moved to Canada as an international student from Turkey. Living alone and missing the flavors of home, he started cooking authentic, spice-filled meals for himself. Soon, friends and classmates began asking for his food, and what started as sharing meals quickly grew through word of mouth. Encouraged by the positive response, he turned his passion for cooking into a business and launched Mr. Spices. Today, our food truck serves fresh, flavorful street food inspired by Turkish and Middle Eastern traditions, bringing people together one delicious meal at a time. 🌶️🚚✨.

Our Mission

our mission is to serve fresh, flavorful, and authentic street food that brings people together. We are committed to creating unforgettable food experiences using quality ingredients, bold spices, and exceptional customer service.
